About

My name is Nicole and I am an avid creator! I enjoy working in multiple mediums and learning new skills and I will share my creative interests here. As a current student, I am balancing course work with my side projects and use creative outlets to relax and destress. My main two creative outlets are crocheting and bullet journaling, though I have been getting into 3D design and my area of study includes frontend design. I started bullet journaling in high school and I began crocheting in my sophomore year of college. I hope to share the progress of my creative work and hope it will spark creative inspo in others.
